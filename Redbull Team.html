MEET THE TEAM

Red Bull Racing is a high-performance Formula 1 team built on the coordination of various expert roles working behind the scenes and on track. The team includes two main drivers who compete in races and provide direct feedback on the car's behavior. The Team Principal oversees overall operations, team strategy, and leadership. Technical performance is handled by engineers specializing in aerodynamics, vehicle dynamics, and power unit integration. Race engineers guide the drivers through every session, while strategy engineers make real-time decisions during races to gain competitive advantages. The pit crew is responsible for lightning-fast tire changes and mechanical maintenance. Simulator and reserve drivers test car setups virtually to support race-day decisions. Every member — from technical staff to logistics coordinators — plays a crucial role in maintaining the team's dominance in Formula 1.
